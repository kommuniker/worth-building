# Code Of Conduct

This project is for rigorous, evidence-based business idea qualification.
That requires direct critique, but it also requires professional conduct.

Expected behavior:

- Be specific and evidence-based.
- Critique ideas, reasoning, prompts, and scripts; do not attack people.
- Respect private business ideas, customer data, and confidential
  assessment output.
- Assume good intent, but fix concrete problems directly.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private ideas, credentials, or customer information.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or PRs away from the concrete change under
  discussion.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block contributors
who violate these expectations.
