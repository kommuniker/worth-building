# Compare Multiple Ideas

Use this after you have at least two completed assessments.

## Goal

Pick one idea to prioritize now.

## Recommended Flow

1. Confirm each idea has a completed assessment and research set.
2. Create a comparison workspace with `scripts/compare-ideas <comparison-name> <idea-a> <idea-b> ...`.
3. Read `core/prompts/workflows/compare-ideas.md`.
4. Score each idea on defensibility, customer urgency, beachhead strength and
   distribution realism, right to win, capital efficiency, speed to proof and
   reversibility, and strategic fit plus opportunity cost.
5. Recommend one priority idea and one immediate validation step.

## Rule

Do not rank ideas only by TAM. A slightly smaller market with better
beachhead, better distribution, better right to win, and a cheaper validation
path is often the correct choice.
