# Financial Scenarios (FICTIONAL EXAMPLE)

> Illustrative only. All figures are invented.

## Scenario Table

| Scenario | Customers | ARPA | Revenue | Runway Months Needed | Main Assumption |

## Interpretation

The idea can be a viable bootstrapped business in the base case. The
worst case is still revenue-positive if churn and support burden stay
controlled, but it would not justify heavy upfront compliance or
enterprise integrations.

## Runway And Funding View

The team should not raise before proof. The RAT and first concierge
pilots are cheap enough to fund directly. External capital would only
make sense after the team proves clinic-owner urgency, design-partner
recruitment, and early note-quality acceptance.
