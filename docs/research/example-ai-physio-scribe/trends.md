# Trends (FICTIONAL EXAMPLE)

> Illustrative only. All figures and trend characterizations are invented.

## Key Trends

1. **Ambient clinical AI became legible to buyers.** Physician-market
   scribes made the idea familiar enough that PT owners do not need to be
   educated from zero.
2. **Administrative load is being reframed as retention risk.** Clinics
   increasingly treat documentation time as a staffing and burnout issue,
   not only a compliance task.
3. **Vertical AI is moving from generic assistants to workflow agents.**
   Products that complete specific regulated workflows can justify higher
   prices than thin transcription wrappers.
4. **Coding and payer pressure keeps documentation quality visible.**
   Better notes can reduce claim friction, which makes the value story
   stronger than time savings alone.

## Implications

The timing supports validation but not automatic build. The team should
test whether PT owners respond to the retention and claims framing before
investing in HIPAA-covered product infrastructure.
